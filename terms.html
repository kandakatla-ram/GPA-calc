What this tool is for

This app exists to help students understand their own grades more clearly — visualizing how each course pulls a GPA up or down, exploring hypothetical "what if" scenarios, and calculating GPA under different scales. It is a study and planning aid, not an official record. Always treat your school's own report card or portal as the source of truth.

Your information isn't stored

When you log in, your StudentVUE credentials are sent once, directly to the API you're using, purely to retrieve your current grades from StudentVUE on your behalf. We don't write your username, password, or grades to any database, log file, or persistent storage. The grades returned are kept only in your browser's session storage — scoped to that one tab — and disappear as soon as you close it or log out.
